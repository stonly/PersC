PersC Chrome Extension

This extension simply lets you keep track of how far along you are on in the web page you're currently viewing.

The percentage number indicates the percentage of the page that you've completed.

The next release will include "Jump To" so you can indicate what percantage you want to jump to in the page and the extension will take you there. Contact me on http://twitter.com/StonlyBaptiste for question/issues/comments and to keep track of future releases.
